How to Be Creative?

January 17, 2025 · 2 min

Creativity is one of the most useful abilities that human posses. Small but important ideas tend to change the things we know in the most unexpected ways possible. Thus, being able to produce these ideas will make you valuable in any place and time.

But there’s a catch. You can’t just create an idea out of thin air. No matter how hard you try, chances are your brain won’t generate anything huge on your request.

How to come up with ideas?#

Unfortunately, there is no ultimate guide for that. Nevertheless there are a lot of tricks to simplify the process.

Build the environment#

Proper surrounding is important. We must construct it in such a way that it encourages the desired behavior, particularly the production of creative ideas.

Spend more time with people who has interesting and diverse thoughts. Try to talk with friends about highly abstract topics and to found the connections with regular things.

Cut out all the unimportant content you consume and let the mind be bored. Constant absorption of easily accessible information prevents your brain from engaging in any deep activity

Explore the problems#

Usually ideas come to solve problems. It’s always easier to work with at least something more than a blank paper.

Look for the inconveniences and obstacles that people have in there daily life. Try to dig deeper into things you do regularly. Turns out there are a lot of inefficiencies and little weird inconsistencies all around us that we’re so used to that we don’t even notice them.

Note thoughts down#

Be brave#

There are people who seem to be fearless, but it is an illusion. Like all humans, they know fear well, they just act despite it. In most cases, you really can’t read of that inconvenient feeling, and you should not to, as it helps to understand

Make it a habit#

As with everything else, the only way to become good at something is to repeat it over and over. And the most pleasent and effective way to do that, is to develop a habit. Make sure to wrap everything you learn in a rapitetive practice.
